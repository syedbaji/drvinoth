TREATMENTS & PROCEDURES

Dr.Vinoth kumar is a senior interventional cardiologist who has expertise in doing the following cardiac procedures. Patients with health conditions that require the following procedures may consult him for the same

PROCEDURES

  1. Angiograms – Coronary, Carotid, Peripheral and Renal.
  2. Angioplasty and stenting(For major heart attack) both emergency and elective.
  3. Complex Angioplasty procedures:
    • Bifurcation stenting
    • Left Main stenting
    • Chronic Total Occlusion(CTO)
    • Rotaablation with stening
    • IVUS and OCT guided stenting
  4. Periperal arterial Stenting, Renal and Carotid artery stenting
  5. Catheter based stenting for failed dialysis Fistula
  6. Pacemakers:
    • Temporary pacemaker Implantation
    • Single and Dual chamber Permanent Pacemaker Implantation
  7. CRT-P/CRT-D /ICD Implantation
  8. ASD,PDA and VSD Device closures
  9. PTMC/PBV
  10. Percutaneous Trans Aortic Valve Implantation (TAVI)

MEDICAL MANAGEMENT

  1. Specialised care for Heart failure patients
  2. Treatment of Uncontrolled BP (Resistant Hypertension)
  3. Management of Heart problems in Kidney failure patients
